Polymarket vs Kalshi — 2026 Comparison

TL;DR: Polymarket has deeper liquidity and more crypto markets. Kalshi is US-regulated and covers more traditional events. MoonX aggregates both — giving you the best of both worlds.

What Is Polymarket?

Polymarket is a decentralized prediction market built on Polygon. It lets anyone trade outcome contracts on future events — from elections to crypto prices to pop culture moments. As of 2026, it hosts over $2B in open interest across thousands of markets. You can browse live Polymarket markets on MoonX.

What Is Kalshi?

Kalshi is a US-regulated, CFTC-designated contract market — the first of its kind. This legal status means it can operate openly in the US, something Polymarket cannot. Kalshi focuses on economic, political, and weather events. See a live example of a Kalshi market on MoonX.

Bitcoin Hits New ATH as Institutional Inflows Surpass $2B in a Single Week

CoinDesk ⏱ March 26, 2026 at 01:30 PM 📖 2 min read
BTC

Spot Bitcoin ETFs recorded their largest single-week inflow since launch, with BlackRock's IBIT alone accounting for over $1.2B of the total $2.1B that flowed into spot Bitcoin products in the past seven days.

What's driving the rally?

Analysts point to a combination of factors: the post-ETF approval effect is still playing out as more registered investment advisors gain approval to hold Bitcoin ETFs, and the Bitcoin halving narrative is beginning to enter public discourse ahead of the anticipated April event. Macro conditions — particularly a weakening dollar index — are also providing a tailwind.

Prediction market outlook

On Polymarket, the probability of Bitcoin reaching $150,000 by June 2026 currently sits at 62%. MoonX aggregates these odds alongside Kalshi and other platforms — you can track live probabilities on the Trending Markets page.

What to watch

Key levels to monitor: $105,000 as immediate resistance (previous ATH from late 2025), and $120,000 as the next psychological target. On the downside, $92,000 has acted as strong support. ETF inflow data published daily by Bloomberg will be the primary signal to watch for continuation or reversal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Bitcoin hitting a new all-time high?
Spot Bitcoin ETFs are driving record institutional inflows. BlackRock's IBIT and Fidelity's FBTC have collectively attracted over $2B in a single week, signaling that traditional finance is allocating to Bitcoin at scale for the first time.
What is IBIT and why does it matter?
IBIT is BlackRock's iShares Bitcoin Trust — the world's largest asset manager's spot Bitcoin ETF. Its inflows are closely watched as a proxy for institutional demand. When IBIT sees large inflows, it typically signals that large allocators are increasing Bitcoin exposure.
What does the prediction market say about Bitcoin's price?
Polymarket currently gives a 62% probability that Bitcoin will reach $150,000 by June 2026. This is derived from real-money bets on both sides of the outcome — not an analyst forecast — and is updated continuously as market conditions change.
